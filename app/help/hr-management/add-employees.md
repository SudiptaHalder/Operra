# How to Add Employees to HR Management

Adding employees to Trezbo's HR Management system is simple and straightforward. Follow this step-by-step guide to get your team set up correctly.

## Step 1: Navigate to Employees Section

From your dashboard, click on the **Employees** tab in the left sidebar. You'll see a list of current employees and an **"Add Employee"** button at the top right.

## Step 2: Enter Basic Information

Click "Add Employee" and fill in the required information:

- **Full Name** - Legal name as per documents
- **Email Address** - Work email for notifications
- **Job Title** - Current position
- **Department** - Select from existing departments
- **Start Date** - Date of joining
- **Employee ID** - Optional internal ID

## Step 3: Add Contact Details

Enter the employee's contact information:

- Phone number
- Personal email (optional)
- Address
- Emergency contact

## Step 4: Set Up Permissions

Choose what the employee can access:

- **Admin** - Full access to all features
- **Manager** - Can approve time-off and view team data
- **Employee** - Can only view and edit own information

## Step 5: Upload Documents

You can upload documents immediately or add them later:

- Contract/Offer letter
- ID proof
- Tax forms
- Certifications

## Step 6: Save and Notify

Click **Save** to create the employee profile. You have the option to:

- Send welcome email
- Assign onboarding tasks
- Add to relevant groups

## Troubleshooting

**Can't find the Add Employee button?**  
Make sure you have Admin or Manager permissions. Contact your account administrator if needed.

**Email not received by employee?**  
Check the email address for typos. You can resend the welcome email from the employee's profile page.

## Next Steps

Once employees are added, you can:

- Set up time-off policies
- Create performance review schedules
- Assign training materials
- Add to company directory
